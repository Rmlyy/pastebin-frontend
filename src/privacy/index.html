Privacy Policy

Effective Date: 9 August, 2023

Thank you for using Rmly's Pastebin ("us", "we", or "our"). We are committed to safeguarding your privacy. This Privacy Policy outlines how we collect, use, and protect your personal information in connection with your use of our pastebin service.

Information We Collect

IP Address

We collect and store your IP address when you view, authenticate, or create a paste on our service. This information is only used for spam detection and prevention purposes. It helps us identify suspicious or malicious activities that may harm our service or compromise the user experience.

Paste Data

When you create a paste on our service, we collect and store the content, name and password of the paste for the duration of its existence. This is necessary to provide the paste-sharing functionality. Once a paste expires it's permanently deleted and its content is removed from our database and cannot be recovered.

Paste Password

If you choose to set a password for a paste, we encrypt the paste's password using the bcrypt hashing algorithm. We do not have access to the actual paste password as it is securely hashed and stored. This ensures that only you have the ability to access the paste content associated with the password.

Use of External Resources

To enhance the functionality and design of our service, we use the following external resources:

  • jsDelivr CDN: We use the jsDelivr Content Delivery Network to improve the loading speed and reliability of our service. This may involve your browser requesting certain resources from jsDelivr's servers, which could result in your IP address being shared with them temporarily. By using our service, you also agree to their Privacy Policy.
  • FontAwesome: We use FontAwesome icons to enhance the visual experience of our service. These icons are loaded from FontAwesome's servers when you access our service. By using our service, you also agree to their Privacy Policy.
  • Cloudflare: We utilize Cloudflare services to enhance the security and performance of our service. Your traffic may pass through Cloudflare's network. By using our service, you also agree to their Privacy Policy.

Hosting

The information within the paste, including its content, name, and password, is held within Oracle Cloud Infrastructure located in Germany. Through your utilization of our service, you recognize and consent to the fact that your data's storage and handling on Oracle Cloud Infrastructure are bound by Oracle's policies.

Data Retention

IP Address

We retain your IP address for an indefinite period of time after your interaction with our service, solely for spam detection and security purposes. We do not use it for any other purpose.

Paste Data

Once a paste expires, it is permanently deleted from our database, and the content cannot be recovered. We do not retain or store expired paste content beyond the period of their existence.

Disclosure of Information

We do not disclose your personal information, including IP addresses and paste content, to any third parties except in the following circumstances:

  • If required by law or governmental authorities.
  • To protect our rights, privacy, safety, or property, as well as the rights, privacy, safety, or property of our users or others.
  • In the event of a merger, acquisition, or sale of all or a portion of our assets, your information may be transferred as part of the transaction, subject to applicable privacy laws.

Your Choices

You can choose to create pastes and use our service anonymously, but please note that your IP address will be collected for spam detection purposes. You can also choose to discontinue the use of our service at any time.

Changes to this Privacy Policy

We reserve the right to update or modify this Privacy Policy at any time. Changes will be effective when we post the revised Privacy Policy on our service. Your continued use of our service after the changes are posted signifies your acceptance of the revised Privacy Policy.

Contact Us

If you have any questions, concerns, or requests regarding this Privacy Policy or our data practices, please contact us at hello@rmly.dev

By using Rmly's Pastebin, you agree to the terms outlined in this Privacy Policy.
